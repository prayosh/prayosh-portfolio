WHO IS BEHIND PRAYOSH?

About Me

I'm Pratik Prajapati, a freelance web designer and custom software craftsman dedicated to converting your online visitors into loyal lifetime customers.

Based in Ranchi, Jharkhand, I founded Prayosh Creations to bring elite, modern, lightning-fast digital solutions to local businesses, shops, and healthcare clinics. I believe that a website shouldn't just be an "online business card"—it should be your absolute hardest-working salesperson, generating leads and building instant credibility 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

My design philosophy is rooted in bold, eye-catching, high-converting layouts that stand out from the endless ocean of boring, cookie-cutter templates. By blending professional 2D Cartoon and Neo-Brutalist design systems with fast loading times and robust logic, I create digital experiences that grab attention in under 3 seconds.
